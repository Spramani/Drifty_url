🕹️ Game Rules & Mechanics

01

Dodge Traffic

You drive along a fast digital cyber highway. Slide, steer, or tilt to change lanes instantly and avoid crashing into other vehicles or road barricades.

02

Collect Coins

Grab neon yellow coins along the asphalt. Each coin adds to your currency stack, allowing you to unlock premium models in the Garage or trigger immediate revives.

03

Gather Nitro Energy

Drive through Nitro power-ups or purchase charges with coins to fill your dashboard gauge. Double-tap or trigger Boost to accelerate, granting double coin values and multiplying your score progression.

04

Risk Close Shaves (Near Misses)

Steer extremely close to passing cars without hitting them to trigger a Near Miss! This increases your combo multiplier up to 5x, boosting your high score dramatically.

Privacy Policy

Your data rights and security protocols

EFFECTIVE DATE: April 21, 2026
APPLICATION: Drifty: Neon Highway Rush (iOS Mobile Game)

Thank you for playing Drifty: Neon Highway Rush. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our mobile game.

1. Zero Data Collection Policy

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) or any other user data. All game progress, high scores, coins, unlocked vehicles, and preferences are stored locally on your device or synchronized using your device's native Apple Game Center functionalities, which are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

2. Apple Game Center & Syncing

If you sign in using Game Center, Apple handles your authentication. We use Apple's API to record your top scores to the leaderboards. We do not transmit or duplicate these credentials on any proprietary servers. Please review Apple's Game Center Privacy Policy to manage your identity.

3. Third-Party Analytics and Diagnostics

Our application may use lightweight diagnostic logs (e.g. Firebase or Apple App Store crash logging) to isolate software errors. These reports are strictly aggregated and contain no personal identifiers, IP addresses, or tracking credentials. No third-party advertising services are integrated that track behavior outside this application.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Because we do not collect any personal data, our game is safe for children of all ages. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by maintaining a strict no-data-collection policy.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.

6. Contact Protocol

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our highway support crew by navigating to the Support & Contact tab on this page or email us directly at: support@spramani-drifty.com.
